poché designs

outside the box. inside the lines.

We are a small architecture firm located in the financial district of San Francisco, California. We strive to have close relationships with our clients and between employees.We draw our lines around our clients’ needs to think outside the box by creating innovative and sustainable designs for the modern human experience. The studio environment allows for collaboration and an abundance of idea exchange. We believe in progress, which means constantly thinking outside the box to gain new perspectives to integrate sustainability to match the ever-evolving state of our world and the people which occupy it. We look to nature for inspiration for both form finding and design outlook, working and researching to understand how humans and their environment can interact symbiotically. We cater to a wide range of clients with a main focus on projects that connect most directly with people. Clients specifically seek out our services for the particular modern style, outlook on the design process, and the prioritization of personal interactions with their clients. We take careful consideration in our research of each specific site and the potential occupants of a space. We strive to create spaces that empower and inspire the users and cater to each client’s particular needs. We take pride in simplicity within our designs to emphasize the human experience within spaces.
